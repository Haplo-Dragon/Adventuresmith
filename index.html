Adventuresmith

This is an Android app for randomly generating elements for tabletop RPGs (names, spells, items, monsters, characters, etc).

I'm not particularly interested in turning this app into a general-purpose random-generator engine -- I don't plan to implement 20+ variations of name-generation tables. I'm interested in more complicated random generation involving multiple dice, interrelated results from multiple tables, etc.

Bugs? Feature Requests? Give me feedback here: https://github.com/stevesea/Adventuresmith/issues

Downloads

I haven't published the app on Google Play yet. To get a copy of the application, go to this link: https://betas.to/guJ4ZNqt

The link will take you to a sign up page where you enter your email. You'll receive an email with a link to download the Crashlytics Beta app.

Once Beta is installed, it'll give you an interface to download my app. Beta downloads my app and then it should take you to the typical APK install screen where you can choose whether to accept the install and be shown the permissions the app is requesting.
